Double-Wall Design:
Revolutionizing the Taco Shell

The design of our taco shell incorporates a two-walled structure (bottom and right), while providing an open, curved space on top for inserting your taco ingredients. By utilizing this structure, the consumer will enjoy a mess-free taco-eating experience. Whether you are on-the-go or just need a quick meal, Taco Back will give you the eating experience you need!

Who is Behind "Taco Back"?

Taco Back was a sudden creation thought of January 2017 by a young woman named Angelia Lane. To bring her product to life Angelia decided to partner up with six other peers in her entrepreneurship class at Georgia Southern University, in Statesboro, Georgia. Michael Farris is the brain behind the technical and social aspects of "Taco Back." Amber Robinson ensures that our product is the best product to our consumer by completing surveys and engaging with our consumers. Dontoria Hitson is the genius of our marketing campaign and making sure you guys know what we are up to. Tyler Fullwood is the man that helped craft us campaigns and our edgy taco shell design. Jadarious Meadows and Rushea Mayo are our dynamic duo that tie up all the loose ends and creative aspects of "Taco Back." These two have worked hard to develop new ideas to the "Taco Back" brand that will launch soon.

Innovation for the Future

Clean: We aim to provide a clean eating experience for our users. Why use an old messy taco shell, when you can use a modern, two-walled shell with optimum design?

Efficient: When you need a good meal quickly, our taco shell will prevent messiness in order to get the food down quicker! Whether you are on-the-go, a student, or a parent looking to feed your kids - Taco Back is for you!

Fast: We have hard-shell and soft-shell options to give you the fastest option you need to create a speedy meal for speedy people.
